Our Vision Meet the Team

Our Vision

To become a nationally and internationally recognized research group in smart and sustainable materials, contributing to climate change mitigation, circular economic implementation, and sustainable development through innovation, interdisciplinary research, and industry engagement.

Our Mission

To foster interdisciplinary, outcome-oriented, and industry-relevant research focused on the development of eco-friendly, resource-efficient, and low-carbon materials, supported by smart and digital tools.

Research Objectives

Strategic goals driving our research agenda

1. Conduct fundamental and applied research on sustainable and low-carbon materials.
2. Develop technologies for valorization of waste into high-value products.
3. Integrate AI, ML, digital twins, and IoT for sustainable materials discovery.
4. Build collaborations with industry, universities, and research centers.
5. Disseminate research through publications, patents, and policy advocacy.
6. Promote circular economy principles through reuse, recycling, and LCA.

Thematic Research Scope

Focus areas driving innovation and impact

Construction Materials

Geopolymers, green cement, coal ash composites, low-carbon building blocks.

Energy & Carbon Materials

Biomass fuels, humic substances, carbon capture & storage materials.

Mining Waste Utilization

Sustainable beneficiation, reuse of tailings, eco-friendly processing.

Life Cycle Assessment

Quantifying environmental and carbon impacts of materials.

Smart & Digital Tools

Predictive modeling, machine learning, big data for optimization.

Water-Energy-Materials Nexus

Predictive modeling, machine learning, big data for optimization.
